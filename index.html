Limited Predictive Power:

A real PRS for a complex trait explains a VERY SMALL amount of the total difference between people. It is as low as 2% to 11% for major disorders.

Ancestry Bias:

The PRS is most accurate for people of European ancestry. Most "discovery GWAS" are in European populations, and thus, the scores are population-specific and less accurate for non-European populations.
